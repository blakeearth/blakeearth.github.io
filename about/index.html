About

Hello! I’m Isaac Blake (blakeearth), a computer science student at New College of Florida and a game developer.

This blog exists as a place for me to jot thoughts and keep records as I learn about and experiment with

  • Free software
  • Game development
  • Decentralization
  • Growing plants
  • Playing the kalimba

I’ve moved content related to my game development to House Mangrove, a new entity I made to separate this personal blog about my learning and hobbies from more technical game development updates.

I’m currently making Homegrown, a multiplayer, open-source game. Its elevator pitch: start on a small, vacant island and grow a little community there with the help of other islanders. Its code is always improving and becoming more readable as I learn game development and software design. It’s written in GDScript and Python.
